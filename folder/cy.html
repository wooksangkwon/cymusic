Chee-young Kim is a musical theatre composer, pianist, violinist and music director who was born and grew up in Seoul, South Korea and now active in New York City. He graduated NYU Tisch School of the Arts with an MFA degree in Musical Theatre Writing. His full length musical Barefoot Persephone (with Sarah Hammond) was awarded a residency at the Johnny Mercer Colony at Goodspeed in February 2015.
  As a music director and musician, he has worked at Musical Theatre Factory, Playwrights Horizons, Play Group Theatre, Centenary Stage Company, 54 Below, among others. He also produced ‘The Seoul of Broadway’ concert at Joe’s Pub in February 2015. Recently he music directed for the musical Call It Courage(by Adam Overette) workshop at Stella Adler Studio.
  Cheeyoung is also a member of Asian American Librettists, Composers and Lyricists Project and the Musical Theatre Factory.
