The Seoul of Broady Way presented at Joe's Pub of Public Theater was the first concert of its kind to celebrate and showcase the work of Korean and Korean-American musical theatre writers living and working in New York City.

Cheeyoung Kim presented one of his songs, Crazy, from the musical ‘Barefoot Persephone (by Sarah Hammond and Cheeyoung Kim)’ in this concert.
